# iTechDMV Website Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

### Visual Language
**Modern Tech + Government Reliability**  
iTechDMV blends the precision of enterprise IT with the clarity of public‑sector technology. The design should feel modern, secure, and efficient — projecting trust, capability, and innovation without unnecessary complexity.

The aesthetic is:
- Clean and minimal  
- Tech-forward with subtle cyber elements  
- Professional enough for government and enterprise clients  
- Accessible and user-friendly  

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Brand Colors
- **iTech Navy (#0A1A2F)** — Core identity color; stability, trust, and technical authority  
- **iTech Teal (#00C2D1)** — Signature accent; innovation, clarity, and modern digital energy  
- **Pure White (#FFFFFF)** — Clean contrast for layouts and typography  
- **Soft Gray (#F2F4F7)** — Neutral backgrounds and section dividers  

### Secondary Colors
- **Slate Gray (#5A6B7A)** — Secondary text and muted UI elements  
- **Success Green (#2ECC71)** — Positive states  
- **Warning Amber (#F1C40F)** — Alerts and cautions  
- **Critical Red (#E74C3C)** — Errors and urgent states  

### Optional Cyber Accents (ThreatSignal Influence)
- **Cyber Blue (#1F8CFF)** — For dashboards, analytics, and security visuals  
- **Matrix Green (#00FF95)** — For subtle tech effects, not primary UI  

---

## Typography

### Font Families
- **Primary:** Inter — clean, modern, highly readable  
- **Secondary:** Source Sans Pro — professional and government-friendly  
- **Display:** Poppins — bold, modern headings with strong presence  

### Hierarchy
- **H1:** 48px, Bold, iTech Navy  
- **H2:** 36px, Semibold, iTech Navy  
- **H3:** 24px, Medium, iTech Navy  
- **Body:** 16px, Regular, Slate Gray  
- **Caption:** 14px, Regular, Slate Gray  

---

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Hero Section
- Gradient blend of **iTech Navy → iTech Teal**  
- Subtle animated mesh or particle field  
- Bold headline with slight glow or gradient text  
- Strong CTA buttons with hover lift  

### Interactive Elements

#### Service Cards
- White cards with soft shadows  
- Teal border or glow on hover  
- Smooth scale transitions  
- Optional icon pulse animation  

#### Navigation
- Fixed header with light blur  
- Teal underline animation for active links  
- Mobile slide-out menu  

#### Forms
- Floating labels  
- Teal focus ring  
- Smooth validation feedback  
- Multi-step progress indicators  

---

## Animation Libraries

### Anime.js
- Page transitions  
- Staggered service card reveals  
- Button hover micro-animations  

### ECharts.js
- Analytics dashboards  
- Performance metrics  
- Security monitoring visuals  

### Splide.js
- Service showcase sliders  
- Testimonials  
- Technology carousels  

---

## Background Effects
- Animated gradient mesh (navy + teal)  
- Low-opacity geometric overlays  
- Optional floating particles for tech theme  

---

## Hover Effects
- **Cards:** 3D tilt + teal glow  
- **Buttons:** Color morph + slight scale  
- **Images:** Zoom + overlay text  
- **Navigation:** Smooth underline + color shift  

---

## Scroll Animations
- Trigger at 30% viewport visibility  
- 100ms stagger  
- Smooth cubic-bezier easing  
- 300ms duration  

---

## Data Visualization Style
- Teal monochrome palette with navy accents  
- Clean, minimal charts  
- Smooth hover tooltips  
- Animated data loading  

---

## Mobile Design Considerations
- Large tap targets (44px+)  
- Swipe gestures for sliders  
- Collapsible navigation  
- Simplified animations for performance  

---

## Accessibility Standards
- WCAG 2.1 AA  
- 4.5:1 contrast minimum  
- Full keyboard navigation  
- Screen reader compatibility  
- Visible focus indicators  

---

## Brand Integration
- iTechDMV logo in header  
- Consistent navy + teal usage  
- Professional imagery (IT, infrastructure, government tech)  
- Trust indicators (certifications, partnerships)  
- Clean, modern, enterprise-ready aesthetic
